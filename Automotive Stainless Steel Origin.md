## FAQ

### Q: What is the difference between AISI 304 and AISI 316?
A: AISI 304 is commonly used for general purposes due to its excellent corrosion resistance and formability. AISI 316 offers even greater resistance to corrosive environments, making it ideal for marine and chemical processing applications.
